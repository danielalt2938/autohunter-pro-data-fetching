    Assembly Instructions
    Elevate your bedroom dresser and add height to a focal wall with this rustic modern mirror. A dual level frame surrounds a smooth, non-beveled mirror, offering a minimalist modern touch to bedrooms. Designed with a 3D paper veneer that gives off a wood grain-like texture along with a two-tone brown and light taupe color palette, this framed mirror makes a striking addition to a lakeside cabin or mountainside chalet. Place this mirror on top of a dresser to transform the space into a meaningful vanity setup. Perfect for your bedroom retreat!
    Includes: One (1) dresser mirror
    - Made with selected 3D paper veneer in brown and light taupe that is easy to maintain
    - The rectangular shape keeps a clean, sharp silhouette
    - A two-tone color palette offers neutral tones for a rustic feel
    - The narrow frame with defines the edge and offers extra dimension
    - The non-beveled mirror offers a smooth reflective surface
